About Waling Guest House

Our Story

Established in 2010, Waling Guest House has been providing exceptional hospitality to travelers from around the world. Our family-run establishment takes pride in offering authentic Nepali experiences while ensuring modern comforts.

Location

Located in the picturesque town of Waling in Syangja district, our guest house is nestled among the beautiful hills of western Nepal. We're conveniently situated just 30 minutes from Pokhara and offer easy access to many local attractions.

Our Services

  • 24/7 reception and security
  • Free Wi-Fi throughout the property
  • Restaurant serving local and international cuisine
  • Tour and trekking arrangements
  • Airport pickup and drop-off services
  • Laundry services
